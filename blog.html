Another important part of his image is training. Ronaldo is famous for physical preparation, body control, and the discipline to repeat the same actions until they become automatic in a real match.

The number 7 also became part of his identity. CR7 is not only a shirt number, but also a personal brand that fans connect with ambition, confidence, and football entertainment.

I chose these moments because they are easy to understand even for people who do not watch every match: big confidence, strong practice habits, and a recognizable style.
